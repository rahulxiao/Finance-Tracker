Expense Categories

Organize your spending with customizable categories

Expense Categorization

Categorizing your expenses helps you understand where your money goes and identify areas where you can cut back. FinanceFlow provides a comprehensive set of predefined categories and allows you to create custom ones to match your unique spending habits.

Add Category

Create a new custom expense category with a unique color and icon.

Manage Categories

Edit, merge, or delete existing expense categories.

Auto-Categorization

Set up rules to automatically categorize transactions based on keywords.

Category Benefits

  • Better Budgeting: Understand exactly how much you spend in each area of your life.
  • Spending Analysis: Identify trends and patterns in your spending habits.
  • Focused Saving: Target specific categories for spending reductions to reach your financial goals.
  • Tax Preparation: Easily identify tax-deductible expenses when filing your taxes.
  • Financial Planning: Make more informed decisions about your future financial needs.
