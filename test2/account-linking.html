Connect Your Financial Accounts

Link your bank accounts, credit cards, investment portfolios, and other financial institutions for automatic transaction import and real-time balance updates. This helps you get a comprehensive view of your finances in one place.

Bank-Level Security

Your security is our top priority. We use bank-level encryption to protect your data, and we never store your banking credentials. All connections are established through secure APIs using read-only access.
